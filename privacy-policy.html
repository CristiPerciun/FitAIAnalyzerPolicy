Privacy Policy

FitAI Analyzer — Last updated: March 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how FitAI Analyzer collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our fitness application.

1. Activity Data Collection

We collect data related to your fitness activities to provide personalized analysis and insights. This data includes, but is not limited to:

  • Distance covered
  • Duration
  • Heart rate
  • Calories burned
  • Steps
  • Elevation and elevation gain

Data is stored securely and used exclusively to process statistics and insights within the app.

2. Garmin Data Usage

FitAI Analyzer integrates Garmin Connect services via the Garmin Health API to import and sync your activities. When you authorize access:

  • We access only the data necessary for app features (activities, metrics, profile)
  • We do not sell or share your Garmin data with third parties
  • We respect the privacy settings and permissions you configure on Garmin
  • Data from Garmin devices is clearly attributed as "Garmin" in the app

FitAI Analyzer uses the Garmin Health API. Garmin data is processed in accordance with the Garmin Connect Privacy Policy and these guidelines.

3. OAuth Login

We use OAuth authentication to securely connect your account:

  • We never store your password
  • We use secure access tokens for connections
  • You can revoke access at any time from your device or provider settings

Login data (e.g. email, username) is used only to identify your account and correctly associate your data.

4. Analytics

We collect analytics data to improve the app and user experience:

  • Feature usage (without sensitive data)
  • Crash events and performance
  • Aggregate and anonymous metrics

This data is not used to personally identify you and is processed in compliance with applicable regulations.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to provide services. You can request deletion at any time; data will be removed within 30 days, except where required by law.

6. Security and Breach Notification

We implement security measures in line with industry best practices (encryption, limited access, monitoring). In the event of a data breach involving Garmin information or our systems, we commit to notifying Garmin within 24 hours and informing affected users in accordance with applicable regulations.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to: access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and objection. You can exercise these rights by contacting us or revoking OAuth access from your Garmin account. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

8. Legal Basis

We process data based on your consent (OAuth) and on contract performance (service delivery). Analytics is based on our legitimate interest in improving the app.
